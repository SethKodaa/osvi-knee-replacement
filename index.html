Knee Replacement

Knee replacement is a procedure carried out to relieve pain and restore function in severely diseased knee joints. Patients typically undergo this treatment when other non-surgical options have failed. The procedure involves replacing damaged joint surfaces with prosthetic components, enabling improved mobility and reduced discomfort.

What to Expect

Patients undergoing knee replacement will typically spend several days in hospital, followed by a period of rehabilitation. Physical therapy is critical to ensure proper joint movement and a successful recovery.
